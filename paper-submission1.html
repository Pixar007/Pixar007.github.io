International Conference on Recent Trends in Data Analytics and Computing Intelligence (ICRTDACI-2025)

April 25-26, 2025

Paper Submission

The authors are invited to submit the Full papers of the Research/Review Papers. The papers will be peer-reviewed and only the accepted and registered papers will be considered for presentation during the conference and publication in the proceedings.
All manuscripts must adhere to the provided template
It is mandatory for at least one of the authors of an accepted paper to register for the International Conference on Recent Trends in Data Analytics and Computational Intelligence 2025 (ICRTDACI-2025) and to present the paper(s) during the conference. Presentations will be conducted through an Offline/Online Mode platform to register for the conference and to present the paper(s), through an Online Mode presentation.

Please Ensure:

Kindly upload the .zip folder containing the following documents:

  • PDF of the revised paper
  • Source files (Word or all LaTeX files)
  • Reviewers' comments response
  • Payment proof (invoice)
